This talk is about how the type-related changes introduced in XPath 2.0 will affect XSLT users. The talk doesn't say anything about XQuery, because there isn't an existing user base of XQuery users who are going to be surprised by the changes. I personally believe that resolving the usability problems encountered by XSLT users will also help XQuery users, but it's clear that they are different communities with different expectations from the respective languages.
