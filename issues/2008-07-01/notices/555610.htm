Appointment of Trustees

In the Sheffield County Court No 370 of 2005

GUSTAFF REINIER UHLENBEEK

(In Bankruptcy)

of 45 Sterndale Road, Sheffield, S7 2LD date of bankruptcy order: 22nd August 2005.

I, Graham Leslie Stuart-Harris of Barber Harrison & Platt, 2 Rutland Park, Sheffield, S10 2PD, hereby give notice that I was duly appointed as trustee of the estate of the above named debtor by the Secretary of State with effect from 19th June 2008. All persons having in their possession any of the effects of the bankrupt must deliver them to me and all debts due to the debtor must be paid to me. Creditors who have not proved their debts must forward their proofs of debt to me.


G L Stuart-Harris, Trustee


25 June 2008.
