Road Traffic Acts

Manchester City Council

TEMPORARY ROAD TRAFFIC REGULATION ORDER

ROAD TRAFFIC REGULATION ACT 1984 - NO TIME LIMIT

ORDER NUMBER 51/1000

Notice is hereby given that the City Council intends, not less than 21 days from the date of this Notice, to make the following Temporary Road Traffic Order.

[51/1000] The City of Manchester (Sherratt Street, Ancoats) (Temporary Prohibition of Vehicular & Pedestrian Traffic) Order 2008. The effect of the Order shall be to prohibit vehicular & pedestrian traffic on Sherratt Street from George Leigh Street to Blossom Street except for accessing those lengths of road which may not (at any given time) be physically affected by the works.

The alternative route for traffic will be via :

1.  Sherratt Street, George Leigh Street & Cotton Street.

2.  Sherratt Street, George Leigh Street & Bengal Street.

The City Council is satisfied that this prohibition is necessary in the interests of public safety during construction and highway surfacing works.

The Order will come into operation on the 25 July 2008 and will remain in force until the order is revoked.


SusanOrrell


City Solicitor


Town Hall


Manchester


M60 2LA
