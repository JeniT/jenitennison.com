Jeni's Schema Pages

These pages are about XML schemas - meta-language for describing the structure of XML documents and validating them. Naturally, there are all sorts of other pages around that cover schemas for XML. Jeni's Schema Pages are dedicated to helping people understand and make the most of using schema languages.
